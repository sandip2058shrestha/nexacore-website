About NexaCore

A Team of Engineers,
Designers, and Strategists

Founded in 2012, NexaCore has grown from a two-person consultancy to a 40-person team delivering technology projects across fintech, healthcare, logistics, and retail. We're based in Sydney but work globally.

We believe great technology is only possible when engineering rigour meets design empathy and business acumen. Every person on our team works across all three disciplines — there are no silos here.
